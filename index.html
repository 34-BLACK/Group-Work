About Us

Luxury Car World was established in 2005 and is a leading high-end car sales platform in China. We have in-depth cooperative relationships with the world's top automotive brands and are committed to providing Chinese consumers with the highest quality luxury models and services.

Our mission is to enable every car owner to enjoy the ultimate pleasure of driving through outstanding products and unparalleled customer experience. Whether you are looking for a business vehicle, a family car or a performance sports car, we can meet your needs.
